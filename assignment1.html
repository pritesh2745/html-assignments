CHARUSAT

Charotar University of Science and Technology (CHARUSAT) has been conceived by Shri Charotar Moti Sattavis Patidar Kelavani Mandal - a not for profit premier education trust of India having a social lineage of more than 123 years. CHARUSAT has been established by the promulgation of Gujarat Private University Act No. 8 of 2009. It is empowered to confer degrees under section 22 of UGC act 1956.

World Wide Web Consortium (W3C)

About W3C

The World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) is an international community where Member organizations, a full-time staff, and the public work together to develop Web standards. Led by Web inventor and Director Tim Berners-Lee and CEO Jeffrey Jaffe, W3C's mission is to lead the Web to its full potential.
